Expand description

Scores a single bid according to a SelectionPolicy.

The resulting value combines three components: price (lower is better), executor reputation, and resources offered by the bid. Each component is multiplied by its weight from the policy and then summed. Bids from executors that cannot afford the price_mana return a score of zero.
